The Doppler effect (also Doppler shift) is the change in the frequency of
a wave in relation to an observer who is moving relative to the source of the wave.

Thus as an observer, if the emitting object is moving towards you, you will observe
higher frequencies (blue-shift), whereas if they're moving away from you, you will observer
lower frequencies (red-shift). ^PeFUataP

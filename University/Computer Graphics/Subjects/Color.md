Anatomy of eyes ^5x7mtpTk

Human eyes are compromised of of rods and cones
rods are more sensitive to waves and only record the lightness
of the light that hits our eyes whereas cones are responsible for
detecting the colors. Cones come in three variations each of
which are sensitive to Red, Green and blue color respectively ^OwYL9Sly

Additive color mixing ^IcqkW72U

The three dimensional color system is inspired by the human visual cone system
Generally color requires three values or dimensions to be represented
which can be then converted to a vector in the color space such that
the magnitude of the vector is its lightness and the direction is the chromaticity

 ^Lb19m6xm

F = rR + gG + bB ^XbCjsOYv

Such that RGB are the primaries of this color space and rgb are psychophysical functions
that can only be determined with experiments ^GZ5A4EDs

There are colors that cannot be generated additively! ^xIBA2Kiv

As such we introduce the subtractive color mixing ^Fwkago68

F = -rR +gG +bB ^kuvSgw4H

This system is linear, meaning multiplying the color
by a scalar K will result in each of the primaries
multiplied by K ^ubodszSb

We can always preform a change of basis
into 3 different primaries ^vTdDgi3D

This allows our model to be represented using barycentric coordinates
since its both linear and additive.
color inside the additive color mixing are inside a triangle, colors using subtractive
color mixing are outside of it ^T1cidAZR
